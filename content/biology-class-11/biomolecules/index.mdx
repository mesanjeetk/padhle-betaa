## NCERT Textbook Questions

<Question>Q1. What are macromolecules? Give examples.</Question>

Macromolecules are large molecules composed of thousands of atoms. They are essential for life and are made up of smaller subunits called monomers.

Examples include:
- Proteins
- Nucleic acids (DNA and RNA)
- Polysaccharides (like starch and cellulose)
